TechMart Electronics - Warranty Policy

STANDARD WARRANTY:
- Laptops: 1 year manufacturer warranty + 6 months TechMart warranty
- Smartphones: 1 year manufacturer warranty
- Accessories: 6 months TechMart warranty
- Smart Home Devices: 1 year manufacturer warranty

WHAT IS COVERED:
- Manufacturing defects
- Hardware failures under normal use
- Battery defects within 6 months

WHAT IS NOT COVERED:
- Physical damage (drops, cracks, water damage)
- Damage from unauthorized repairs
- Normal wear and tear
- Software issues caused by user

WARRANTY CLAIM PROCESS:
1. Contact support with order ID and issue description
2. TechMart technician will diagnose remotely
3. If hardware defect confirmed, product collected for repair
4. Repair or replacement completed within 7-10 business days

EXTENDED WARRANTY:
- TechMart Shield Plan available at checkout
- Covers accidental damage for 1 additional year
- Price: Rs. 999-2999 depending on product value
