TechMart Electronics - Shipping Policy

DOMESTIC SHIPPING:
- Standard Delivery: 3-5 business days (Free above Rs. 499)
- Express Delivery: 1-2 business days (Rs. 149)
- Same Day Delivery: Available in Bangalore, Mumbai, Delhi, Chennai (Rs. 299)

ORDER TRACKING:
- Tracking link sent via SMS and email after dispatch
- Track orders at techmart.com/track or TechMart app
- Real-time updates every 4 hours

ORDER PROCESSING:
- Orders placed before 12PM dispatched same day
- Orders after 12PM dispatched next business day
- Orders on Sundays/holidays dispatched next working day

INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING:
- Available to 25 countries
- Delivery time: 7-14 business days
- Customs duties charged as per destination country
- DHL and FedEx used for international orders

FAILED DELIVERY:
- 3 delivery attempts made
- After 3 failed attempts, order returned to warehouse
- Customer must contact support to redeliver or get refund
