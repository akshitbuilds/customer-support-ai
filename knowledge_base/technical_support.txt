TechMart Electronics - Technical Support Guide

COMMON LOGIN ISSUES:
- Forgot password: Click "Forgot Password" on login page, OTP sent to registered mobile
- Account locked: After 5 failed attempts, account locked for 30 minutes
- Two-factor authentication: Settings > Security > Enable 2FA

APP INSTALLATION GUIDE:
- Android: Download from Google Play Store, search "TechMart"
- iOS: Download from App Store, search "TechMart"
- Minimum requirements: Android 8.0+ or iOS 13.0+
- App size: 45MB Android, 52MB iOS

ORDER RELATED ISSUES:
- Order not showing: Check registered email/mobile, allow 10 minutes after placing
- Payment deducted but order not placed: Amount auto-refunded in 24-48 hours
- Unable to apply coupon: Check expiry date and minimum order value

DEVICE SETUP HELP:
- Laptop setup: techmart.com/setup/laptop
- Smartphone transfer: Use TechMart Transfer app for data migration
- Smart home setup: Follow QR code in package or visit techmart.com/smarthome

ERROR CODES:
- Error 404: Page not found, clear browser cache
- Error 500: Server issue, try after 10 minutes
- Payment Error PE001: Card declined, contact your bank
- Payment Error PE002: UPI timeout, check UPI app and retry
