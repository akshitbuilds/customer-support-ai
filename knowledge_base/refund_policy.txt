TechMart Electronics - Refund Policy

ELIGIBILITY FOR REFUND:
- Products must be returned within 7 days of delivery
- Item must be unused, undamaged, and in original packaging
- All accessories, manuals, and warranty cards must be included
- Refund requests must be initiated through the TechMart app or website

NON-REFUNDABLE ITEMS:
- Software products once activated
- Items damaged by customer misuse
- Products without original packaging after 48 hours of delivery

REFUND PROCESS:
1. Submit refund request on website or app
2. TechMart team will review within 24 hours
3. Pickup scheduled within 2-3 business days
4. Refund processed within 5-7 business days after pickup

REFUND METHODS:
- Original payment method for card/UPI payments
- Bank transfer for COD orders (3-5 business days)
- TechMart wallet credit (instant)

PARTIAL REFUNDS:
- Opened box items: 10% restocking fee applied
- Missing accessories: deducted from refund amount
